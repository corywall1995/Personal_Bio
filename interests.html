My Interests

Interest and Hobbies:

   One of my interests is soccer; I've been playing soccer for as long as I can remember. I played club ball up until high school where I tried out for and made the varsity team as a freshman and became a starter my sophmore year. In my senior year I was given the honor of Team Captain also during my senior year I damaged some ligaments in my right knee that prevents me from playing much anymore. I tried to switch to coaching but it just wasn't for me. Though I do not play much or coach anymore I do still watch it regularly. My favorite club is Manchester United FC (an English club), and if I had to choose a second favorite it would be FC Bayern Munich (a German club).

   Another hobby I have is reading. My favoite author at the moment is Christopher Paolini, the author of the Inheritance Series (Eragon, Eldest, Brisingr, Inheritance). I have read every book in that series numerous times. Other series and authors I read often are The Oregon Files by Clive Cussler, Percy Jackson and the Olympians/ Heroes of Olympus by Rick Riordan, and The Presidential Agent by WEB Griffen. I'm also a history buff and love to read and learn about the past.

   I have also been known to enjoy gaming. I do most of my gaming on console (PS4), I play on console for many reasons, the main one being that my current laptop can't handle most modern games and building a gaming PC is expensive. I play wide variety of games ranging from FPS's to RPG's. I'm not very good at most video games, but I still play them and find them entertaining.
