Interest and Hobbies

   One of my interests is soccer. I've been playing soccer since I could walk. I played club ball up until high school where I became a starter in only my sophmore year and in my senior year i was given the honor of Team Captain. During my senior year I damaged some ligaments in my right knee that prevents me from playing much anymore. I tried to switch to coaching but it just wasn't for me, though I do not play much or coach anymore I do still watch it regularly, mainly European fixtures, I can't stand watching American soccer.

   Another hobby I have is reading. My favoite author at the moment is Christopher Paolini, the author of the Inheritance Series (Eragon, Eldest, Brisingr, Inheritance). I have read every book in that series numerous times. Other series and authors I read often are The Oregon Files by Clive Cussler, Percy Jackson and the Olympians/ Heroes of Olympus by Rick Riordan, and The Presidential Agent by WEB Griffen. I'm also a history buff and love to read and learn about the past.

   I have also been known to enjoy gaming. I do most of my gaming on console (PS4), I play on console for many reasons, the main one being that my current laptop can't handle high detail graphics and building a new gaming PC is expensive. I play wide variety of games ranging from FPS's to RPG's. though I'm not very good at most video game I still play them and find them entertaining.
